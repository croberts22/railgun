Synopsis

Special booklets bundled with the first press release of the 1st and 2nd Blu-ray and DVD volumes of Onimonogatari anime. They contain short advertisement manuscripts published in Yomiuri Shinbum along with short kakioroshi.

Volume 1
1. Tsubasa Board
2. Mayoi Castle
3. Shinobu Science
4. Suruga Palace

Volume 2
1. Nadeko Mirror
2. Hitagi Salamander
3. Yotsugi Future
4. Ougi Travel
